Mission Guide

Each level has a mission target. Destroy enough hostile devices to clear the level.

  • Protect all city buildings. If buildings are destroyed, you lose.
  • Use your Iron Dome launcher to intercept missiles.
  • Starting Wave 3, a Laser Dome unlocks. Click the battery or press Tab/1/2 to switch active weapons.
  • Friendly F-35 jets and the carrier can help intercept threats.
  • Keyboard: Space shoot, P pause, N new game, M mute.
